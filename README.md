🔍 Key Insights

Some important insights discovered during the analysis include:

Customers with month-to-month contracts have higher churn rates.

Long-term contract customers are more likely to stay.

Customers with higher monthly charges show increased churn risk.

New customers are more likely to leave during the first few months.

These insights help businesses focus on retaining high-risk customers.

💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following strategies can help reduce churn:

✅ Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts
✅ Offer loyalty rewards for long-tenure customers
✅ Provide discounts for high monthly charge customers
✅ Improve customer onboarding experience
✅ Create targeted retention campaigns for new users
